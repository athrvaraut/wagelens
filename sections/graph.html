Algorithm 06 — Graph Theory / BFS

Org Hierarchy Salary Flow

Model the organization as a directed weighted graph. BFS traversal visits each node level-by-level, computing the total payroll per department. Hover over nodes to see salary details.

⏱ BFS/DFS: O(V + E)  |  Space: O(V)  |  V = employees, E = reporting lines

Algorithm 04 — Divide & Conquer

Payroll Processing Tree

Divide the org into two halves (Dept A & B). Recursively compute sub-payrolls independently, then merge totals upward — identical to Merge Sort's conquer+merge phases.

⏱ T(n) = 2T(n/2) + O(n) → O(n log n) by Master Theorem (Case 2)
