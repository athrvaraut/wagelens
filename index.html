Your Tax — Explained Completely
When, why, how much, and where your tax goes
📅
WHEN is tax deducted?
Every month when your salary is processed — your employer deducts TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) before crediting your account. You never handle it — it's automatic.
🏛️
WHY do you pay tax?
Tax funds public infrastructure — roads, hospitals, defence, schools. India uses a progressive slab system: higher income = higher rate. It's your contribution to the nation.
🔢
HOW is it calculated?
Not on your full salary — only on "taxable income". Gross salary minus Standard Deduction (₹50,000) = taxable income. Then each slab rate applies only to income within that slab.
💰
WHERE does it go?
Central Government (80%) + State Government (20%). Used for MGNREGA, PM-KISAN, infrastructure, defence, education, healthcare subsidies and public services.
